Features

Built for daily kidney care

Blood pressure

Blood glucose

Track mg/dL readings with context so patterns are easier to spot over time.

Weight

Offline-first

Vitals logging always succeeds locally. Sync reconciles with the cloud when online.

Patient & caretaker accounts

Separate signup flows for patients and caretakers, with secure authentication.

Privacy on sign-out

Local health data is wiped when you sign out, so shared devices stay safe.

Why offline-first

Your readings should never depend on signal.

Clinic visits and daily life do not guarantee connectivity. A cloud-only app fails the moment the network drops. Kidney Care keeps a local database as the source of truth so every reading is saved immediately.
